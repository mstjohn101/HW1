Who Is This Guy?

My name is Matt St. John and I was born in Olympia, Washington on May 8th, 1989. I attended the University of Washington and graduated in 2011. Upon graduation, I worked for Amazon for 5 years doing technical business development. I had a great time working on new technology such as Fire TV, Fire Phone (rest in peace) and Amazon Echo. I have a lovely girlfriend and best friend, Sophie (see Matt's Best Buds), and a cat named Cat.
